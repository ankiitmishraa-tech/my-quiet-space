Introvert Hoon Main

March 3, 2024 5 min read 24 likes

Log kehte hain - "Itna kam kyun bolte ho?", "Party mein kyun nahi aate?", "Milte julte kyun nahi?"

Par unhe kya pata... main apni duniya mein kitna khush hoon. Chai ki pyali, ek acchi kitaab, aur meri apni sochein - bas yahi meri duniya hai.

Introvert hona kya hai?

Introvert hona koi bimari nahi, yeh ek personality hai. Hum soche samjhe bolte hain, hum observers hain, hum apne aap mein complete hain. Bahar ki duniya mein shor hai, lekin humein sukoon milta hai andar ki khamoshi mein.

Jab log party karte hain, hum ek kitaab ke saath time spend karte hain. Jab log group mein baatein karte hain, hum ek do logon ke saath gehri baatein karte hain. Jab log social media pe busy hain, hum apne khayalon mein khoye rehte hain.

Meri khamoshi, meri taakat

Dheere dheere maine samjha - yeh khamoshi hi meri taakat hai. Main dekh sakta hoon jo log nahi dekh pate. Main sun sakta hoon jo awaazein log nahi sun pate. Main mehsoos kar sakta hoon woh cheezein jo log ignore kar dete hain.

Aaj itna hi. Phir milenge apni isi quiet space mein. 👋

Khamoshi mein bhi ek awaaz hoti hai, bas sunne wala chahiye.
